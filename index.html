How to Update TomTom N14644 Maps

The TomTom N14644 is a widely used GPS device that requires regular map updates to ensure accurate navigation. Updating your device helps you get the latest routes, traffic information, and points of interest.

Why Update Your TomTom N14644?

  • Access newly constructed roads and route changes
  • Stay informed about speed limits and traffic updates
  • Ensure reliable performance and device stability
  • Get updated POIs such as gas stations, restaurants, etc.

What You Need

  • Your TomTom N14644 GPS device
  • USB cable for PC connection
  • Computer with internet access
  • TomTom MyDrive Connect installed

Steps to Update TomTom N14644 Maps

  • Connect your TomTom N14644 device to your PC via USB
  • Download and install TomTom MyDrive Connect software
  • Launch the software and sign in to your TomTom account
  • The system will check for updates automatically
  • Click “Update” to install the latest maps and firmware
  • Wait until the update completes before disconnecting the device

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure the USB cable is working properly
  • Use a stable and fast internet connection
  • Disable VPN or firewall temporarily during update
  • Restart your computer and device if not recognized

For the smoothest navigation and up-to-date directions, it's recommended to update your TomTom N14644 maps every 3–6 months.
